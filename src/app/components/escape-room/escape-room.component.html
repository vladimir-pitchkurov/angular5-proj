Rules & Safety

For your safety and others, Adrenaline Entertainment Centers asks you to follow these rules when participating in our escape room games.

Read all rulles

  1. You have 60 minutes to escape the room. All necessary clues and puzzles to escape the room are within the room itself.
  2. Please use your creativity, critical thinking, and teamwork to solve the clues and puzzles. Do not use physical force to solve any clues or puzzles. All puzzles and clues can be solved with creativity, critical thinking, and teamwork.
  3. Please do not take objects or souvenirs from the room. These objects are required for future participants.
  4. Please do not bring alcohol or come to our escape rooms while under the influence of another substance.
  5. We will monitor the room at all times with security cameras to ensure safety and fair play of our participants.
  6. Please do not take pictures or videos while in the room.
  7. Please do not copy ideas, clues, puzzles, or room designs that you see while participating in one of our rooms.
  8. With your consent, we will ask to take a picture of you and your group after you have participated in the room so that we can share your experiences on social media. All participants have the right to refuse this option.
  9. Participants enter our escape rooms under their own risk. Adrenaline Rush will not be held liable.
  10. In the event of an emergency, there is an unlocked door that necessitates swift exit or entry.
  11. Participants may be asked to leave if their actions are deemed unsafe or inappropriate, so please follow the rules outlined above
